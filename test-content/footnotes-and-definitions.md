## Definition lists (extended syntax)

Term 1 : Definition for term 1

Term 2 : First definition for term 2 : Second definition for term 2

Complex term : A definition that contains **bold text**, `inline code`, and continues on the next line.

## Abbreviations (extended syntax)

The HTML specification is maintained by the W3C.
